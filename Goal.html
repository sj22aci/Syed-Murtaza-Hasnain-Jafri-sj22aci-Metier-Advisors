Goal

The idea is to make a career counselling application for people that require guidance for their future.

The goal of this mobile application is for the students who want to start their career and dont know what to do.

This app will help them connect with counsellors, which then will guide them on what to do next. This app will show latest news, offers that can be availed.

This app will also be for people who are qualified as counsellors and are looking for a job. This will allow them to register on this app as counsellors.
